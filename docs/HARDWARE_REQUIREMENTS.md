# Minimum Hardware Requirements

### Baseline

- **CPU:** 4-core CPU (8+ core CPU Recommended)
- **RAM:** 8 GB (16+ GB Recommended)
- **VRAM:** 0 GB (4+ GB Recommended)
- **Storage:** 5 GB

### OSB Inpainting (Flux Models)

- **FLUX.2-klein-4B-SDNQ:**
  - **VRAM:** +5 GB (Drops to ~1.5-2.5 GB with Low-VRAM mode)
  - **RAM:** +6 GB
  - **Storage:** +5.2 GB

- **FLUX.2-klein-9B-SDNQ:**
  - **VRAM:** +8 GB (Drops to ~2.5-3.5 GB with Low-VRAM mode)
  - **RAM:** +9 GB
  - **Storage:** +12.0 GB

- **FLUX.1-Kontext-dev (Nunchaku Backend):**
